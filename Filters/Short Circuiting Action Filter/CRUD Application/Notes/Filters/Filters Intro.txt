Filters:the methods that execute before and after the action method execution
-->they execute as a part of Filter pipeline -->executes after the action selector executes

as soon as a particular action method is selected --> it executes the filter pipeline

Filter pipeline-->collection of filters , some execute before the action , some after

ex: Authorization Filter-->have only 1 method that executes BEFORE
ex:Action Filter -->2 methods one executes BEFORE , and the other is AFTER
